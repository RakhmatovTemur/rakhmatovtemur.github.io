    Interpretation

    • Total real spending rises steadily across each 20-year block, with the steepest acceleration between 2000 and 2024.
    • Teacher/instructor salary remains the largest category, but its share is gradually lower than in the 1960s as the system broadens support functions.
    • Student support and technology spending become clearly visible after 2000, reflecting expansion beyond core classroom salary costs.

    Estimation Notes

    Historical totals are anchored on available Arkansas current expenditure reporting points and translated into 2024 dollars using approximate CPI-U factors. Category-level splits are estimated to remain consistent with reported structure in later years and adjusted where categories emerge in the modern period.

    The chart is intended for trend interpretation, not audited accounting. For official fiscal reporting, use original state and federal statistical releases.
